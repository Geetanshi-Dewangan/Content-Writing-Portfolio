The Truth Behind Popular Skincare Misconceptions

  • Myth #1: Oily Skin Doesn’t Need Moisturizer
    Skipping moisturizer makes skin produce more oil. Use a lightweight, non-comedogenic moisturizer.
  • Myth #2: Expensive Skincare Works Better
    Drugstore products often have the same active ingredients as luxury brands.
  • Myth #3: Natural Ingredients Are Always Safe
    Not all natural ingredients suit every skin type. Always patch-test!
  • Myth #4: You Only Need Sunscreen on Sunny Days
    UV rays penetrate clouds and windows—daily SPF is a must!
